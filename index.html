About Me

A Tutor Who Genuinely Cares

I believe every student can excel at mathematics — with the right guidance and genuine encouragement.

My tutoring style is built on patience, strong conceptual clarity, and the belief that students learn best when they truly understand the "why" behind mathematics.

Rather than drilling methods, I help students become independent problem-solvers who can tackle unfamiliar exam questions with confidence. I identify gaps quickly through on-the-fly questions, then adapt my explanations in real time to meet each student exactly where they are.

I also connect maths to real-world applications — engineering, data, finance, everyday decisions — because students are more engaged and motivated when they see that mathematics is meaningful and alive beyond the classroom.

Teaching Approach

The Way I Teach

Four principles that guide every lesson

Teach to Fish

My goal is not just to solve today's problem — it's to equip students with the understanding and mental flexibility to solve tomorrow's. I emphasise deep conceptual mastery, not rote memorisation.

Diagnose First

I identify learning gaps quickly through targeted questions, then adapt my explanation in real time. Once I understand exactly where a student is stuck, I address that gap directly and move forward.

Custom Questions

I create original problems tailored to each student's specific weaknesses. Standard textbook exercises rarely expose individual misconceptions — targeted custom practice does.

Real-World Links

I connect maths to engineering, data, finance, and everyday decisions. When students see why maths matters, they become genuinely curious, more engaged, and far more motivated.
