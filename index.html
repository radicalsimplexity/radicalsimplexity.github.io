About

Why Choose Lester?

Patient, conceptual, and genuinely invested in every student's growth

Caring & Encouraging

I create a genuinely supportive space where students feel safe to make mistakes and ask questions freely — without pressure or judgement. Rebuilding confidence is just as important as getting the right answer.

Friendly & Approachable

Students often tell me I don't feel like a typical tutor. Lessons are warm, conversational and low-pressure — making it easier to stay curious, open up, and engage deeply with the material rather than just trying to get through it.

Trustworthy & Professional

With 20+ years of experience, qualifications from NUS and Georgia Tech, and a consistent track record of significant grade improvements — students and parents can rely on a structured, proven approach to learning.

I help students move beyond memorising steps to genuinely understanding mathematics — and that shift makes all the difference in exams and beyond.

With more than 20 years of tutoring experience across Secondary, JC and early university mathematics, I combine diagnostic questioning, original custom problems, and real-world applications drawn from my industry background in engineering, finance and data analytics — to build confident, independent problem-solvers.

  • Teach to fish — build understanding that transfers to new and unfamiliar questions
  • Diagnose first — find the exact gap through targeted questions, then address it in real time
  • Custom problems — original questions designed for your specific misconceptions
  • Real-world links — connect maths to engineering, data, finance and everyday decisions
